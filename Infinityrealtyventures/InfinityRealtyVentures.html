About

Founded in 2017 by Haji Chakralwale, Mumbai based Infinity Realty Ventures provides general contracting, construction management and pre-engineered building services to a broad range of commercial and industrial clients. Anchored by loyal employees who are dedicated to providing superior service, and grounded in the philosophy that honesty and integrity are as important as the bottom line, Infinity Realty Ventures has enjoyed an impeccable reputation for over 3 years. We have never failed to complete a project. We have done all type of building work and we do work in Labor, with material and Lock-key. Infinity Realty Ventures is a company that has built a strong reputation for delivering a quality service, on time, and at a competitive price.
